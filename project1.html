PoaHub – Multi-Channel eCommerce Platform

  • Live Site
  • Source Code (Private)

PoaHub is a robust, full-featured B2C and B2B eCommerce platform designed to connect retailers and wholesalers with a central inventory service. The application supports guest checkouts, user wishlists, product reviews, and dynamic filtering based on categories, countries, deals, and availability.

As a Full Stack Developer (backend-focused), I was responsible for designing scalable RESTful APIs, integrating third-party services, managing product inventory via microservices, and deploying the backend using Docker and systemd on a production VPS environment.

Key features include:

  • ✅ Central inventory service with retail & wholesale segmentation
  • ✅ Product filtering by category, IP/country, and promotional status
  • ✅ Guest & authenticated checkout flow with invoice email generation
  • ✅ Wishlist, product reviews, and recently viewed product tracking
  • ✅ Admin dashboards and CMS-style banner management
  • ✅ Deployed with Docker & Nginx, secured via SSL using Certbot
